# How To Take A Shower

## During Shower

1. Brush tooth first
1. Shampoo
1. Wash hair
1. Wash ear
1. Optionally use conditioner
1. Body wash
1. Optionally use face cleanser

## After Shower

In no particular order 

- dry feet
- use lip balms
- use cotton buds
- apply body lotion
- dry slippers
- apply face cream
