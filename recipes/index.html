    European Soft Bread
    bread flour 200g
    water 140g
    yeast 4.4g
    salt 3g
    oil 16g
    sugar 10g
    • first proof: 35 minutes in oven 40 degree
    • second proof: 25 minutes in oven 40 degree
    • bake for 25 minutes 180 degree

    蒸鸡蛋糕

    Pizza Dough
    all purpose flour 250g
    water 175g
    salt 2g
    olive oil 12.5g
    yeast 1g
    • 5-6 minutes in oven with the highest temperature
